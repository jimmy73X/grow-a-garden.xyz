Grow your own garden in Roblox! Plant seeds, watch them bloom, and harvest profits while offline. Join the fun and flex your fruits!

Play Now on Roblox

Game Features

Plant & Grow

Choose from dozens of seeds to plant and watch them grow into beautiful plants and fruit trees. Each plant has unique growth patterns and requirements.

Garden Tycoon

Build your gardening empire! Expand your land, hire helpers, and automate processes to maximize your profits even while offline.

Community

Join thousands of gardeners! Visit friends' gardens, trade rare seeds, and participate in community events and competitions.

Achievements

Complete challenges and unlock achievements. Show off your gardening skills with rare decorations and exclusive items.

Offline Progress

Your garden keeps growing even when you're away! Come back to harvest ripe fruits and collect your earnings.

Customization

Personalize your garden with hundreds of decorations, paths, and structures. Create your dream garden layout!
